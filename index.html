Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping

What countries do you ship to?

We currently only ship within the UK. To enquire about shipping to a different destination, please contact us.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Standard DPD shipping normally takes 2 days. Orders placed on Fridays and over the weekend will be collected the following Monday.

Returns and Refunds

How do I return a product?

Items must be returned within 14 days after receiving your order. Items must be returned in the same condition in which they were received and be unworn/unused, have any tags still attached, and include all the original packaging.

How long will it take to receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 7 days from when we receive the item(s).
